== Description ==

A sidebar widget that enables your users to send free text messages using a small sidebar widget within your own wordpress blog. Works with any cellular phone in the Unites States and Canda

**Features**<br />
-includes built-in widget<br />
-add your own header and bottom text
-enables any of your blog visitors to send text messages from their computer to any cellular phones in the United states and Canada.

**Future Releases**<br />
*email me or visit promoteseo.com

== Installation ==

1. Download the plugin
2. Unzip send-a-text-message.zip
3. Upload the entire mobilecs folder to your plugins directory
4. Go to the Plugins page in your WordPress Administration area and click to 'Activate' "send a text message" on WordPress.
5. Go to Appearance->widgets and add the "send a text message" sidebar widget on WordPress widget to your sidebar

Please let me know any bugs, improvements, comments, suggestions.
